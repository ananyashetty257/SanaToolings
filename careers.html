Engineering
Tool Designer
New

Experience: 3-5 years
Location: On-site
Department: Engineering

We're looking for an experienced Tool Designer to join our engineering team. You'll be responsible for designing precision tools and fixtures for manufacturing processes.

Responsibilities:
  • Design tools, dies, and fixtures for manufacturing processes
  • Create detailed technical drawings and specifications
  • Collaborate with production teams to optimize designs
  • Troubleshoot and resolve design issues
Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering or related field
  • 3-5 years experience in tool design
  • Proficiency in CAD software (SolidWorks preferred)
  • Knowledge of manufacturing processes and materials
Production
CNC Operator

Experience: 2-4 years
Location: On-site
Department: Production

Seeking skilled CNC operators for our manufacturing facility. You'll be responsible for setting up, operating, and maintaining CNC machines to produce precision components.

Responsibilities:
  • Set up and operate CNC machines
  • Monitor production processes and make adjustments as needed
  • Perform quality checks on finished components
  • Maintain equipment and work area
Requirements:
  • 2-4 years experience operating CNC machines
  • Ability to read and interpret technical drawings
  • Knowledge of machining processes and tooling
  • Strong attention to detail and problem-solving skills

New

Location: On-site
Department: Quality Control
